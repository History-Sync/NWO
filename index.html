Electric Railroad Bridge / Interurban Bridge

A 3D Scan of the electric railroad bridge, which connects Wood County to Lucas County across the Maumee river. This bridge was built in 1908, making it two years older then Bowling Green State University. The bridge is no longer used and is currently in a state of decay.

You can press the play button to see the bridge from other angles.

Would you like to know more? You can read more about this bridge on Wikipedia.
